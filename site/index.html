FAQ

Do I need an API key?
Not to start — sign in and you get free managed asks. For unlimited free use, paste your own Anthropic key in the extension's Options.
Is my content private?
With your own key, content goes straight to Anthropic and never reaches us. On managed plans we relay the request to Anthropic but never store your prompts or answers — we count requests, not text. See our Privacy Policy.
What's the verification layer?
A one-tap re-check of an answer against live web sources, scored claim by claim with links — so you can trust what you act on.
How do I cancel?
Subscriptions are billed through Paddle; cancel anytime and you keep access until the period ends.
